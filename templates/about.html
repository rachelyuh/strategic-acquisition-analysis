So… what is Acquivision?

Acquivision is a merger model platform that simplifies the complex and tedious mergers & acquisitions process into three simple user entries. A merger model’s goal boils down to one: if company A acquires/buys company B, will the earnings per share of company A increase or decrease? By utilizing several vast APIs and a plethora of algorithms designed by our team, we can come to this conclusion with breakneck speed. Now, the question is: how accurate is our model? Would the EPS from a standard excel spreadsheet model be vastly different from Acquivision’s platform? The question is, as with most things, it depends. There are acquisitions that are heavily dependent on cost and revenue synergies that are impossible to account for due to their complex and difficult to quantify nature. What Acquivision excels at is providing a succinct and easy to digest model purely based on the financials of two companies for our clients.

What’s important to note is that no company will ever buy another company because a model projects that it will see a significant EPS increase. What that increase will show, however, is that the opportunity is something to look into. Acquivision makes it possible for professionals to see the rough estimate of change in EPS from an acquisition and then decide whether it’s an opportunity worth pursuing.
